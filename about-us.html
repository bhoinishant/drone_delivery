About Dronella

Dronella is an innovative company focused on R&D in automation of aerial services and making a system that delivers medicines through drones. It is one of the leading “delivery through drone” company in India. The Company is incorporated in 2020 and offers a wide range of UAV (drone) based services in the domain of Aerial Delivery.


The salient feature of UAV delivery is that during this pandemic where the world needs to break the chain of this virus ,we have started a drone delivery system where in you can place an order and it will be delivered by drone system. It develops an unique platform as there will be no direct contact with the delivery person and it will be safe and your parcel will be delivered in a much lesser time as compared to the existing methods. We will be joining hands with several supply chain management companies to enhance Aerial Delivery platform for future business opportunities pertaining to delivery.


We are unique at our service as we deliver through drones. It is a zero contact delivery as there will be no delivery boy to be in contact with. Directly your order will be picked from the medical shop and delivered to you. This delivery will be made in much less time as expected because no roaming here and there in search of address.
